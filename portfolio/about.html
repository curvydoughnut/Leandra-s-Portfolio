About me

Leandra Pearson is a Creative Product Designer and AI builder based in Atlanta, originally from Queens, NY. She studied Graphic Design at Wood-Tobe Coburn and spent a year at Ringling College of Art and Design before launching her career as a Graphic Designer creating visual communication for brands. After realizing she wanted to solve real-world problems beyond static screens, she transitioned into UX/UI design, teaching herself through hackathons, YouTube, and connecting with other design professionals.

Today she's passionate about solving problems with design and building exciting digital experiences. Her certifications include Enterprise Design Thinking and AI Fundamentals from IBM, plus UX/UI and Figma training from SkillUp. Outside of work, she's an anime enthusiast (loves Witch Hat Atelier, Jujutsu Kaisen, Demon Slayer, and The Apothecary Diaries), enjoys museum exhibits, writes short stories, goes to concerts across many genres, and just generally loves having a good time.
